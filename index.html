About Us

Reliable Bookkeeping,
Right Here in Davao

ADDS and Bookkeeping Solutions Co. is a full-service bookkeeping and accounting firm based in Dumoy Toril, Davao City. We are dedicated to helping small and medium enterprises, sole proprietors, and corporations maintain accurate financial records and stay fully compliant with Philippine tax laws.

Our team of certified professionals combines deep local expertise with modern accounting practices to deliver timely, reliable, and affordable financial services tailored to your unique business needs.

  • ✔ Strict BIR and SEC compliance
  • ✔ Confidential and secure data handling
  • ✔ Personalized service for every client

Payroll Processing

BIR Tax Filing

  • Withholding tax compliance
